"Making the world a better place, through open source"

Web Development

Mobile Development

Covers topics such as framework improvements, bug hunting, better development of applications and so on.

Creative Design

Covers topics such as intellectual property, better development of useful content as well as graphic and motion design softwares.

Mathematical Computing

Covers issues such as the fibonacci series, mathematical algorithms and better computational code.

General

These projects cover general techlonogy related issues ranging from everyday use to industrial level.
